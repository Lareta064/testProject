CryptoArtMuseum was founded by an international team of professionals from different spheres: crypto, art, design and business. Our aim is to show that NFT is not just a hype, that it is a new stage of art history which gives new opportunities to both artists and art lovers.

We are all passionate about innovations and want to make them useful for the development of our society.

Our project is not just an online museum, we plan to support talented artists, educate collectors and make a contribution to the heritage of our generation.

The future is now, don’t stop the world's progress.

CryproArtMuseum foundation is a non-commercial organisation to help NFT art to develop as a full-fledged sphere of art.

We plan to aggregate donations to support young artists, give them grants and organize residence with outstanding experts in digital art.

We make all spendings transparent and will give our contributors all info about our achievements and plans.

If you are also passionate about NFT art, feel free to donate and subscribe to our newsletter to know all updates and success stories.
